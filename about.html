About Us

“Team Table” online game was developed by EVENTIK.

Eventik is a new generation event management and organization company that provides event services for corporate projects such as motivation & teambuilding activities, marketing and advertising activities within the scope of internal and brand communication activities of corporate companies. We have both online and physical game options for our corporate customers.

The general aim of Team Table game is to be able to take a ball from START point to FINISH point with team directions on a moving table with a challenging labyrinth on the floor.

The team leader directs his/her teammates by giving voice commands during the game. Except for the leader, all team players (who are kind of blindfolded) will see the game scene blurred and try to take the ball from START point to FINISH point by following the team leader's commands. Among all teams, the team that reaches the finish in the shortest time will be the champion of the day.
