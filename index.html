Rules
  • Since the microphone is active in the GAME, you need to turn off the microphone in ZOOM when you open the game. Please check before starting the game.
  • In Team Table, you compete against time, not against a single opponent. The team that completes the game at the shortest time will be the winning team of the day. Therefore, all teams are your opponents. If you cannot finish the game within the specified maximum time of the event, you will be out of the race.
  • At the main screen, you must find your TEAM NAME (which you already know) from the list and join the game channel of your team. After the team selection, you wait for all of your teammates to come to the game and you can start the game when the START button is active.
  • 1 TEAM LEADER of all teams must be determined before the game and this person must indicate himself/herself as the leader at the team's gathering screen, by pressing 'Become Leader' button.
  • The game will not start until each participant in all teams is active in the system. When everyone is ready, the member of each team will click on the READY button then the game will start at the same time for all teams.
  • The game screen will initially open as a normal screen for everyone, including the team leader, so that all participants will get used to the game system at this easier part. When the players arrive at the 2nd flag point / checkpoint, the members other than the team leader, will now see the screen blurry and continue the game with the directions of the team leader. Since the game gets harder after the Second Flag point, it is important that the team leader manages the team and the teammates cooperate successfully.
  • The team leader directs his/her teammates by giving voice commands during the game. Except for the leader, all team players (who are kind of blindfolded) will see the game scene blurred and try to take the ball from START point to FINISH point by following the team leader's commands. When the ball reaches the FINISH point at the specified number of times (normally 1 time), the system stops the team's time and saves it as a list.
  • To move the table, team members must use the arrow keys (such as UP-DOWN) on the keyboard. The team leader cannot use the keyboard, only can command other members to press keys.
  • If you are the team leader, when you want an edge of the table to be lifted, you need to command the person on that side of the table, so that the ball will move correctly. When you need to lift the top right of the table, you should give the 'Up' command to your friend whose name is there.
  • When team players want to see the game in normal mode, they can remove the blur effect by pressing on EYE-shaped button or “Q” on keyboard and gain the right to see the normal screen for 10 seconds. As a team, each team member has 10 rights to remove the blur mode and see the normal screen.
  • When you press the eye button and see the normal screen, you will not be able to hear the other participants' voice.
  • Including the team that finished the slowest & last, after all teams complete the game, the LEADER BOARD will be displayed on the screen in 2 minutes. All teams will see a 2 minutes count down which will start at the same time for everyone.. The CHAMPION TEAM and TOP 5 TEAMS will be listed on the score board.
  • When your internet is disconnected or your pc shuts down for some reason, you must re-enter the game within 3 minutes. If you cannot connect within this period, you will be disconnected from the game and you won't be able to continue the rest of the game. Other team players can continue the game without you.
